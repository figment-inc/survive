## Clip 01 (0:00-0:07) — The Hook
NARRATOR: A million people asleep. Twenty-three seconds. You would not want to be in Tangshan, 1976.

## Clip 02 (0:07-0:14) — Setup
NARRATOR: Tangshan is an industrial city — one million people in unreinforced brick. No steel frames. No flex. At 3:42 in the morning, you are asleep.

## Clip 03 (0:14-0:21) — Escalation
NARRATOR: A magnitude 7.6 quake strikes directly beneath the city. Unreinforced walls don't flex — they shatter. Your ceiling is brick and mortar, and there is no give.

## Clip 04 (0:21-0:28) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Everything drops. You are pinned. Dust fills your lungs. You scream. Dust fills your mouth. The voices around you go quiet.

## Clip 05 (0:28-0:35) — Twist Payoff
NARRATOR: Two hundred and forty thousand dead — nearly all of them in bed. The earthquake lasted twenty-three seconds. The buildings lasted less.
