## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Chicago's newest theatre just passed every safety inspection the city requires. You would not want to be inside on December 30th, 1903.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're in one of nineteen hundred seats — a Wednesday matinee packed with women and children.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A stage light ignites a curtain above you. The asbestos safety curtain jams halfway down — and a backdraft funnels straight into your face.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You're one of 602 dead in fifteen minutes — inside a fireproof building where every safety feature was decoration.
