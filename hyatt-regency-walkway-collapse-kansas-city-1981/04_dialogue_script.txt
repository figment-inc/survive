## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Two thousand people are dancing in the Hyatt Regency lobby. Above you, two concrete walkways hang from steel rods. You would not want to be in Kansas City, 1981.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're the engineer who signs off on a change: two shorter rods instead of one continuous rod through both walkways. That doubles the load on a single connection.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You don't recheck the math. The contractor builds it. The city inspector signs off. The hotel opens to the public.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You are one of 114 people killed by a design change no one reviewed — the deadliest structural collapse in American history, because one bolt carried twice what it was meant to hold.
