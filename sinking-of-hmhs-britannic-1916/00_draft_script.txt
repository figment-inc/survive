## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: After the Titanic, they redesigned everything — bigger lifeboats, watertight doors, a double hull. The Britannic sinks even faster. You would not want to be on the Britannic, 1916.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: She's a hospital ship in the Aegean Sea. A mine tears open the hull at breakfast. The captain orders full speed toward shore to beach her.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: That decision kills people. Open portholes scoop the sea inside. The faster she moves, the faster she floods.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Two lifeboats launch early. The still-spinning propellers drag them in and shred them.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The unsinkable design lasted fifty-five minutes. Thirty people died because the ship that fixed everything still couldn't outrun an open window.
