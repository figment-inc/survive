## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: After the Titanic, they redesigned everything — bigger lifeboats, watertight doors, a double hull. The Britannic sinks even faster. You would not want to be on the Britannic, 1916.

## Clip 02 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Each knot of speed pushes more sea through the open portholes onto your deck.

## Clip 03 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You climb into a lifeboat launched too early. The still-spinning propellers drag it backward.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The unsinkable design gives you fifty-five minutes. Thirty people die because the ship that fixed everything still can't outrun an open window.
