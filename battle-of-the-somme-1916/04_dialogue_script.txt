## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your generals spent a week shelling German trenches — but the shells are duds. The wire isn't cut. You would not want to be at the Somme, 1916.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're told the plan is simple: artillery destroys everything, then you walk across and take the trenches. But the Germans dug thirty feet down, and your shells barely scratch them.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The shelling stops. You climb out. Officers tell you to walk in a straight line. The enemy is dead.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Twenty thousand British soldiers died on the first day — walking upright into guns that were never supposed to be firing.
