## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A chunk of foam hits Columbia's wing during launch. Engineers ask for satellite photos to check the damage. NASA management says no. You would not want to be on Columbia, 2003.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The foam punched a hole in the heat shield — the only thing between you and 3,000-degree re-entry plasma. No one looked, so no one knows.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: For sixteen days you orbit Earth. Engineers email concerns. Managers close the discussion — if the shield is damaged, there's nothing you can do anyway.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You hit the atmosphere. Superheated gas pours through the hole. The left wing burns from the inside. Columbia breaks apart over Texas at Mach 18.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Seven crew members died. Three engineers asked for a closer look. Management decided the foam wasn't a concern.
