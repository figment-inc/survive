## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Grain ships from Egypt carry rats into Constantinople's harbor. The rats carry something worse. You would not want to be in Constantinople, 542 AD.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The city imports all its food by sea. Every sack of grain that feeds you brings infected fleas onto the docks.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Swellings appear in your groin and armpits. Within days, five thousand people are dying — every single day.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The gravediggers are dead. Bodies are stuffed into towers, loaded onto barges, and dumped at sea.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Emperor Justinian could have closed the harbor. He kept the grain ships coming — because a starving capital scared him more than a sick one.
