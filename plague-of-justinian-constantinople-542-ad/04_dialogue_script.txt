## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Grain ships from Egypt carry rats into Constantinople's harbor. The rats carry something worse. You would not want to be in Constantinople, 542 AD.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The city imports all its food by sea. Every sack of grain that feeds you brings infected fleas onto the docks.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Swellings appear in your groin and armpits. You could seal the harbor — but the grain ships are the only thing keeping you alive. Five thousand people die every single day.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You beg to close the harbor. Justinian refuses — a starving capital scares him more than a sick one.
