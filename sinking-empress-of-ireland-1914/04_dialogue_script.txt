## Clip 01 (0:00-0:07) — Hook
NARRATOR: The Titanic took three hours to sink. Your ship goes down in fourteen minutes — and most of the portholes are open. You would not want to be on the Empress of Ireland, 1914.

## Clip 02 (0:07-0:14) — Setup
NARRATOR: It's a warm May night, so passengers sleep with their portholes open. A coal freighter appears out of the fog and hits you broadside.

## Clip 03 (0:14-0:21) — Escalation
NARRATOR: The ship rolls onto its side. Water pours through a thousand open portholes. You can't climb to the lifeboats — the deck is now a wall.

## Clip 04 (0:21-0:28) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Cabin doors swing shut and pin you inside. The river is four degrees. You have minutes.

## Clip 05 (0:28-0:35) — Ending
NARRATOR: A thousand and twelve people died — most trapped in their beds. The ship carried enough lifeboats. Almost none were launched.
