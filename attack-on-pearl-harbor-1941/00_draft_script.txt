## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The radar operator sees incoming planes fifty miles out. His officer tells him to ignore it — they're expecting friendly bombers. You would not want to be at Pearl Harbor, 1941.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Sunday morning. Your battleships are lined up side by side, engines cold. Ammunition lockers are sealed shut per peacetime regulations.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: 183 Japanese planes hit the harbor at once. You can't move the ships, and you can't reach the bullets to fight back.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The Arizona's magazine explodes. A thousand men die in nine seconds. Oil burns across the water — you swim through fire.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: 2,403 Americans killed. Someone saw them coming and was told not to worry.
