## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: You would not want to be a radar operator at Pearl Harbor, December 7th, 1941. You pick up incoming planes fifty miles out — your officer says stand down, the Army is expecting friendly B-17s from the mainland.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You look out at eight battleships packed side by side, engines cold, ammunition lockers sealed under peacetime regulations.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A hundred and eighty-three Japanese planes hit the harbor at once. You can't move the ships, and you can't reach the bullets to fight back.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: 2,403 Americans killed. You saw them coming. You were told not to worry.
