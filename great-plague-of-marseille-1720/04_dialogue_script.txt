## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A ship docks carrying plague rats. You watch merchants convince officials to skip quarantine — the silk will spoil. You would not want to be in Marseille, 1720.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Within weeks, the dockworkers are dead. Authorities seal the old quarter with walls and armed guards — trapping you inside with the infected.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You sleep inches from the dying. Bodies pile faster than anyone can bury them, and the rats cross every wall.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The merchants saved their silk. Half the city — fifty thousand people — paid for it.
