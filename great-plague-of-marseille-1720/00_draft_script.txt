## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A ship's cargo is crawling with plague rats. The city's merchants convince officials to skip quarantine — the silk will spoil. You would not want to be in Marseille, 1720.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Within weeks, the dockworkers are dead. Authorities seal the old quarter with walls and armed guards — trapping you inside with the infected.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You can't leave. Bodies pile faster than anyone can bury them. The rats don't care about walls.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Plague spills into every district. Convicts are forced to clear corpses — a thousand bodies a day stacked in open pits.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The merchants saved their silk. Half the city — fifty thousand people — paid for it.
