## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Port Royal is built on a sand spit. The buildings are brick — heavy, European brick, on loose sand. You would not want to be in Port Royal, 1692.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: It's the richest port in the Caribbean. Warehouses, taverns, two thousand buildings packed onto a strip of land barely above sea level.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: An earthquake hits and the sand liquefies. Heavy brick doesn't float — it sinks. Whole streets drop into the ground with you still inside.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The sea rushes in behind. You're chest-deep in liquid sand when the wave buries you.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Two-thirds of the city vanished in three minutes. They built a stone town on sand — and the sand swallowed it whole.
