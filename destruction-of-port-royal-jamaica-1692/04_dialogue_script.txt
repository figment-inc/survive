## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Port Royal is built on a sand spit — heavy European brick on loose, wet sand. You would not want to be in Port Royal, 1692.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You live in the richest port in the Caribbean. Two thousand buildings packed onto a strip of land barely above sea level, and you're inside one of them.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: An earthquake hits and the sand liquefies beneath you. Heavy brick doesn't float — it sinks. Whole streets drop into the ground with you still inside.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Two-thirds of the city vanished in three minutes. Port Royal was the richest port in the Caribbean — and you built every brick of it on sand that couldn't hold.
