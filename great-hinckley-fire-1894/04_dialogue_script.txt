## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Four hundred lumber mills strip Minnesota bare. The scraps pile across the forest floor, and a drought turns it all to kindling. You would not want to be in Hinckley, 1894.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Small fires smolder for weeks. You don't put them out — burning slash is how mills clear land.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The fires merge around you. You feel the wind reverse — superheated air pulling oxygen at hurricane speed. Trees explode before flames touch them.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The mills left the waste. You left the fires burning. A town built by lumber, erased by what lumber left behind.
