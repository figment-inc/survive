## LINE-LEVEL CRITIQUE

### You-As-Subject — 7/10

**Failing line (Clip 01):** "Four hundred lumber mills strip Minnesota bare. The scraps pile across the forest floor, and a drought turns it all to kindling."
**Problem:** Three sentences, zero "you." The hook is strong as a premise but the viewer is watching a nature documentary for the first 8 seconds. "The scraps pile" and "a drought turns" are both third-person constructions. This is forgivable in Clip 01 because the framing line follows, but it's the reason this isn't a 9.
**Fix:** Trim to keep the premise but thread "you" in sooner: "Four hundred lumber mills strip Minnesota bare. You walk over the scraps every day — and a drought turns it all to kindling."

**Failing line (Clip 03):** "Trees explode before flames touch them."
**Problem:** This is a spectacular detail but "you" disappears for the final beat of the escalation clip. The viewer shifts from participant to observer right when immersion matters most.
**Fix:** "The trees around you explode before flames touch them." — One word. Keeps the viewer inside the firestorm.

### Word Count — 7/10

**Problem:** 94 words. Four words over the sweet spot ceiling. Not critical but at ~2.6 wps that's an extra 1.5 seconds that could push past the Shorts window.
**Fix:** Clip 05 is the place to trim. "The mills left the waste. You left the fires burning. A town built by lumber, erased by what lumber left behind." → Cut "The mills left the waste." — it's redundant with Clip 01. Open the ending on "You left the fires burning." This drops you to ~87 words and sharpens the accusation.

### Gut Punch — 7/10

**Failing line (Clip 05):** "A town built by lumber, erased by what lumber left behind."
**Problem:** This is intellectually satisfying irony but it releases the physical tension from Clip 04. The viewer just had scorched lungs and a departing train — then the ending gives them a thesis statement. The stomach unclenches. The brain takes over.
**Fix:** Keep the irony but anchor it in a body: "You left the fires burning. Four hundred people couldn't outrun what the mills left behind." — Scale embedded, human cost present, the irony still lands but it's attached to death, not abstraction.
