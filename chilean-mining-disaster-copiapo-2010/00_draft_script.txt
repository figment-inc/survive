## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A mine collapses and traps thirty-three men seven hundred metres underground. The company has no rescue plan. You would not want to be in Copiapó, 2010.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're deeper than two Eiffel Towers stacked. The ventilation fails. The emergency shelter has two days of food for ten people.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You ration two spoonfuls of tuna every forty-eight hours. Seventeen days pass before a drill bit breaks through — just to confirm you're alive.

## Clip 04 — Turn
NARRATOR: Rescue drills keep snapping in the rock. Engineers estimate months. You watch supplies drop through a hole the width of a grapefruit.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The company skipped reinforcing the access tunnels to save money. You spent sixty-nine days buried because maintenance cost too much.
