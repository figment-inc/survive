## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Vesuvius has been quiet for five hundred years. You built your home on slopes of old lava you don't even recognize as lava. You would not want to be in Naples, 1631.

## Clip 02 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Mud fills your doorway in seconds. You reach the shore. The sea is gone — debris from the eruption shoved it back. Then it returns.

## Clip 03 — Ending
NARRATOR: You built your churches with stone quarried from the last eruption's lava. Four thousand people were buried under the next one.
