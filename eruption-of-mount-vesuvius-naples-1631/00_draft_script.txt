## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Vesuvius has been quiet for five hundred years. Farmers built villages right up to the crater. You would not want to be in Naples, 1631.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Centuries of silence convinced everyone the volcano was dead. Churches and homes sit on slopes made of old lava.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The eruption sends boiling mudflows down every river valley. You can't outrun liquid rock following the roads you built.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Mud buries towns to their rooftops. The sea pulls back, then returns as a wave.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Four thousand people lived on that mountain because five centuries of silence told them it was safe. The mountain remembered.
