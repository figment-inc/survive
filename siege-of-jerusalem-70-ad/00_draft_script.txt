## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Rome offers to let you surrender. Your own zealots burn the city's food supply to force everyone to fight. You would not want to be in Jerusalem, 70 AD.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Three factions are at war inside the walls before Rome even arrives. They torch granaries that could have fed the city for years.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Four Roman legions seal every exit. You're starving — not because Rome cut off your food, but because your own leaders destroyed it.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: People boil leather. Soldiers tear open the Temple walls stone by stone. You have nowhere to run.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Rome didn't need to starve Jerusalem. The defenders did it themselves — then wondered why no one was left to hold the walls.
