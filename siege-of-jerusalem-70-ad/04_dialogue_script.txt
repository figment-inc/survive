## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Rome offers terms. Your own zealots torch every granary in the city. You would not want to be in Jerusalem, 70 AD.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're fighting factions inside the walls before you face the legions outside. Half your defenders are dead — killed by each other.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Four legions seal every gate. Your ribs press through your skin — not because Rome cut your food, but because your own leaders burned it.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Rome didn't starve you. You did it to yourselves — and no one's left to hold the walls.
