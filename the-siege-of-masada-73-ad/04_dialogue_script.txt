## Clip 01 (0:00-0:08) — The Hook
NARRATOR: Rome sends eight thousand soldiers to kill nine hundred people on a rock. When they finally break through, no one is left to kill. You would not want to be at Masada, 73 AD.

## Clip 02 (0:08-0:16) — Routine
NARRATOR: You sharpen a blade on the cliff edge every morning. Four hundred metres straight down. The wind tastes like dust and salt from the Dead Sea.

## Clip 03 (0:16-0:24) — False Safety
NARRATOR: Herod built this fortress a century before you arrived. Cisterns, storerooms, a bathhouse. Your children have never known any other home.

## Clip 04 (0:24-0:32) — The Detail
NARRATOR: Eight thousand legionaries camp on the plain below. They do not try to climb. They start carrying earth — one basket at a time.

## Clip 05 (0:32-0:40) — Escalation
NARRATOR: Months pass. The ramp grows. Jewish slaves haul the dirt and stone. You watch your own people build the road to your door.

## Clip 06 (0:40-0:48) — The Turn
NARRATOR: A battering ram rolls up the ramp on wooden rails. It hits the wall. You brace timber behind the stone — Rome sets it on fire.

## Clip 07 (0:48-0:56) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The wind blows the flames back toward the Romans. Then it shifts. Your last wall burns.

## Clip 08 (0:56-1:04) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The breach opens at dusk. Rome pulls back for the night. You have until dawn.

## Clip 09 (1:04-1:12) — The Human Gesture
NARRATOR: Ten men are chosen by lot. Each family lies down together. The ten walk from room to room.

## Clip 10 (1:12-1:20) — Aftermath
NARRATOR: One man remains. He checks every door, sets fire to the storerooms, and falls on his sword.

## Clip 11 (1:20-1:28) — The Number
NARRATOR: At dawn, Rome enters the fortress and finds silence. Nine hundred and sixty dead. Two women and five children — hiding in a cistern.

## Clip 12 (1:28-1:36) — Callback
NARRATOR: The fortress never fell. Everyone inside was already gone.
