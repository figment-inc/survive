## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: You live below the Yellow River. Engineers built its dikes higher than your rooftop — so when they break, the river doesn't flow down a valley. It buries your village. You would not want to be on the Yellow River, 1887.

## Clip 02 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You watch cracks spread through the dikes after weeks of rain. You send for repair funds — the court denies them.

## Clip 03 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The south bank collapses. A wall of yellow mud swallows eleven hundred villages before you can reach high ground.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You were one of nine hundred thousand drowned by a river lifted above you — one unfunded repair at a time.
