## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Scientists publish a hazard map showing your Colombian town will be destroyed by a volcano. Your government calls it alarmist. You would not want to be in Armero, 1985.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You live below a glacier capping the volcano. If it erupts, melted ice and ash will form a river of concrete moving sixty kilometers per hour — down the valley where you live.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Officials tell you to stay calm. You never see the map. On November thirteenth, the volcano erupts.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Twenty-three thousand people died because a government dismissed a map that predicted exactly this — and never showed it to you.
