## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Scientists warn that Nevado del Ruiz is about to erupt and a mudflow will bury the town below. Officials tell you to stay home. You would not want to be in Armero, Colombia, 1985.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The volcano sits under a glacier. When it erupts, melted ice mixes with ash and becomes a river of concrete moving sixty kilometers per hour.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A hazard map showing Armero in the red zone was published weeks ago. The government called it alarmist. You never saw it.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The lahar hits at night while you sleep. Three meters of mud swallow your entire town in minutes.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Twenty-three thousand people died. The map was right. They just didn't show it to you.
