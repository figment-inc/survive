## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: You're asleep in a stone building with no steel frame. At 5:20 a.m., a 7.1 earthquake hits Messina. You would not want to be in Messina, 1908.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Your home is centuries-old stone — heavy walls, no reinforcement. The city has never updated its building codes. When the ground shakes, the walls collapse inward, onto your bed.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You dig with bare hands, but the rubble is massive. Then the sea pulls back — and a twelve-metre tsunami rolls through the ruins.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You're one of eighty thousand. You never woke up — and no one is coming for three days, because officials refuse to believe the reports.
