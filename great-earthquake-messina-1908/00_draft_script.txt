## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A 7.1 earthquake hits Messina at 5:20 in the morning — while the entire city is asleep in unreinforced stone buildings. You would not want to be in Messina, 1908.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Sicilian homes are heavy stone walls with no steel framing. They stand fine until they shake. Then they collapse inward — onto the beds.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Survivors dig with bare hands, but the rubble is massive. Then the sea pulls back — and a twelve-metre tsunami rolls through the ruins.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You survived the quake trapped under stone. Now seawater floods the gaps where you're breathing.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Eighty thousand people died — most never woke up, and rescue ships weren't sent for three days because officials couldn't believe the reports.
