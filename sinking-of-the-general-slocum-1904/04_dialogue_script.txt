## Clip 01 (0:00-0:07) — Hook
NARRATOR: A steamboat full of families catches fire on the East River — and every safety device on board is fake. You would not want to be on the General Slocum, 1904.

## Clip 02 (0:07-0:14) — Setup
NARRATOR: A thousand women and children on a church picnic. Fire breaks out below deck, and you grab a life preserver — it crumbles, filled with cork dust.

## Clip 03 (0:14-0:21) — Escalation
NARRATOR: The fire hoses fall apart when you turn them on. The captain doesn't stop — he speeds up, and the wind fans the flames across the entire deck.

## Clip 04 (0:21-0:28) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You jump holding your child. Your wool dress drags you under. No one in this neighborhood was ever taught to swim.

## Clip 05 (0:28-0:35) — Ending
NARRATOR: You are one of over a thousand dead in fifteen minutes — close enough to shore that people on the sidewalk watched. Every lifejacket, every hose, every inspection was a lie.
