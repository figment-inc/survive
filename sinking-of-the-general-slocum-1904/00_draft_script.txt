## Clip 01 (0:00-0:07) — Hook
NARRATOR: A steamboat full of families catches fire on the East River. Every safety device on board is rotted or fake. You would not want to be on the General Slocum, 1904.

## Clip 02 (0:07-0:14) — Setup
NARRATOR: A thousand women and children are on a church picnic. Fire breaks out below deck. You reach for a life preserver — it crumbles in your hands, filled with cheap cork dust.

## Clip 03 (0:14-0:21) — Escalation
NARRATOR: The fire hoses fall apart when you turn them on. The captain doesn't stop — he speeds up, and the wind fans the flames across the entire deck.

## Clip 04 (0:21-0:28) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Mothers jump holding children. Their wool dresses drag them under. Almost no one in this neighborhood was ever taught to swim.

## Clip 05 (0:28-0:35) — Ending
NARRATOR: Over a thousand people died in fifteen minutes, close enough to shore that people on the sidewalk watched. Every lifejacket, every hose, every safety inspection had been a lie.
