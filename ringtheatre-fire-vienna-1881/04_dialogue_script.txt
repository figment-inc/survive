## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Vienna's Ringtheatre has no electric lights — the stage is lit by open gas flames inches from painted canvas. You would not want to be in Vienna, 1881.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're in the balcony when a stagehand's lamp catches a backdrop. In seconds, fire climbs into the fly tower above the stage.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You watch the safety curtain drop — and jam halfway. Fire pours over it straight toward you.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: At least 384 people burned alive around you. The curtain that was supposed to save everyone had never been tested — not once.
