## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Vienna's Ringtheatre has no electric lights — the stage is lit by open gas flames inches from painted canvas. You would not want to be in Vienna, 1881.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: A stagehand tries to light a lamp. The flame catches a backdrop. In seconds, fire climbs into the fly tower above the stage.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The safety curtain jams halfway down — it was never tested. Fire pours over it into the auditorium.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The gas lines are still on, feeding the flames. Exit doors open inward — the crowd crushes against them and they won't move.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: At least 384 people burned. The curtain that was supposed to save them had never been dropped once.
