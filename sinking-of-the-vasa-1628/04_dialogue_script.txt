## Clip 01 (0:00-0:08) — The Hook
NARRATOR: The mightiest warship ever built sails for twenty minutes and never leaves the harbor. You would not want to be on the Vasa, 1628.

## Clip 02 (0:08-0:16) — Routine
NARRATOR: You are a shipwright in Stockholm's royal yard, swinging an adze into green oak until your shoulders burn — shaping ribs for a vessel meant to terrify the Baltic.

## Clip 03 (0:16-0:24) — The Detail
NARRATOR: You mount sixty-four bronze cannons across two gun decks. Every order the king sends — more guns, higher castles, more gilt — adds weight above the waterline.

## Clip 04 (0:24-0:32) — The Warning
NARRATOR: Thirty men run back and forth across the deck for a stability test. The ship rolls so violently they stop, or she will capsize at the dock.

## Clip 05 (0:32-0:40) — The Silence
NARRATOR: You report it up the chain. The admiral says nothing — the king is abroad, expecting his flagship. You do not write the letter that says she is not safe.

## Clip 06 (0:40-0:48) — The Day
NARRATOR: August tenth. Families are aboard for the maiden voyage. You cast off in light wind, the pitch-smell of fresh planks under a clear sky.

## Clip 07 (0:48-0:56) — The Turn
NARRATOR: Thirteen hundred metres out, a gust hits the sails. The deck tilts. You grab a stay line. Cold water rushes across your boots —

## Clip 08 (0:56-1:04) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: She does not come back. Water fills the gun deck. The cold hits your chest like a wall. Your hands find nothing to hold.

## Clip 09 (1:04-1:12) — Human Gesture
NARRATOR: You surface and cling to wreckage, and the whole city watches from the waterfront.

## Clip 10 (1:12-1:20) — Aftermath
NARRATOR: Thirty souls are lost. At the inquiry, you blame the admiral. The admiral blames the king. The king blames God.

## Clip 11 (1:20-1:28) — The Number
NARRATOR: You built the most powerful warship on earth, and she drew less than five feet of freeboard.

## Clip 12 (1:28-1:36) — Callback
NARRATOR: You built her to terrify the Baltic. She never left the harbor, because no one dared terrify the king.
