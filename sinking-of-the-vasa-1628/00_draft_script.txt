## Clip 01 (0:00-0:08) — The Hook
NARRATOR: The mightiest warship ever built sails for twenty minutes. She never leaves the harbor. You would not want to be on the Vasa, 1628.

## Clip 02 (0:08-0:16) — Routine
NARRATOR: You are a shipwright in Stockholm's royal yard. For two years, you have shaped oak ribs for a vessel meant to terrify the Baltic.

## Clip 03 (0:16-0:24) — The Detail
NARRATOR: Sixty-four bronze cannons across two gun decks. The king wants more guns, higher castles, more gilt. Every order adds weight above the waterline.

## Clip 04 (0:24-0:32) — The Warning
NARRATOR: You know something is wrong. Thirty men run back and forth across the deck as a stability test. The ship rolls so violently they stop — or she will capsize at the dock.

## Clip 05 (0:32-0:40) — The Silence
NARRATOR: The admiral watches. He says nothing. The king is abroad, waging war, expecting his flagship. No one writes the letter that says she is not safe.

## Clip 06 (0:40-0:48) — The Day
NARRATOR: August tenth. Crowds line the waterfront. Families are aboard — wives, children, guests for the maiden voyage. You cast off in light wind.

## Clip 07 (0:48-0:56) — The Turn
NARRATOR: One thousand three hundred metres from the dock, a gust catches the sails. The Vasa heels to port. Water pours through the open gun ports —

## Clip 08 (0:56-1:04) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: — and she does not come back. The weight holds her down. The Baltic floods the lower deck in seconds. You hear timber groan, then silence.

## Clip 09 (1:04-1:12) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: She sinks in thirty-two metres of water, masts still above the surface, flags still flying. Around you, people who cannot swim cling to wreckage in full view of the city.

## Clip 10 (1:12-1:20) — Aftermath
NARRATOR: Thirty souls are lost. At the inquiry, every man blames the man above him. The shipwright blames the admiral. The admiral blames the king. The king blames God.

## Clip 11 (1:20-1:28) — The Number
NARRATOR: She carried more firepower than any warship on earth. She was top-heavy by design, unsafe by committee, and launched by silence.

## Clip 12 (1:28-1:36) — Callback
NARRATOR: You built her to terrify the Baltic. She never left the harbor, because no one dared terrify the king.
