## Writing Guidelines

- **Voice on frame 1.** Clip 01 starts with "What happens if..." — ZERO seconds of silence.
- **Present tense ONLY.** "You wake up" not "You've arrived."
- **Short sentences.** Average 5-8 words. Punches of 3-4 words. Never exceed 15 words.
- **Second person throughout**: "you", "your" — the viewer IS the skeleton.
- **Agency verbs**: "You run", "You grab", "You shout" — not passive observation.
- **Failure beats every 10-15 seconds**: try and fail. The retention engine.
- **One philosophical beat per episode**: a moment of insight amid chaos.
- **Cliffhanger ending**: never wrap up neatly. End on a haunting image.
- **Dark humor through understatement**: "You are having a very bad day" — not mockery.
- **Recognizable anchors**: reference things viewers know — famous people, artifacts, pop culture.
- **Historical accuracy**: real dates, real numbers, real consequences. Every fact checkable.
- **Total word count**: 175-215 words across all 10 clips.
- Narration in this script MUST exactly match the dialogue direction in video prompts.
