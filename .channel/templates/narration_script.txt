## Writing Guidelines

- Every clip except the establishing shot gets narration
- Hook in clip 02 — "So. You've just arrived in..." or variant
- Second person throughout: "you", "your", "you've just..."
- Each fact should make the viewer think "oh no, it gets worse"
- Build dread through historically accurate details — real numbers, real names, real consequences
- Peak horror at The Catastrophe — the narrator delivers the worst details clinically
- End with a gut-punch fact followed by a wry sign-off
- Dark humor through understatement: "suboptimal", "considerably worse", "not ideal"
- Max 3-4 dark-humor phrases per episode, placed at key moments
- Keep it conversational — contractions, short sentences, spoken storytelling cadence
- Real victims are treated with respect — the skeleton absorbs all comedic punishment
