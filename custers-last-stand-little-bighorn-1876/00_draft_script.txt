## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Two hundred sixty soldiers ride into a valley holding two thousand warriors. Their commander split the regiment into three pieces. You would not want to be at Little Bighorn, 1876.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Custer wants to attack before the village can scatter. He divides his force without knowing the camp stretches three miles along the river — the largest gathering of Lakota and Cheyenne in a generation.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Your column charges downhill. Warriors pour from every direction. You're driven back to a ridge with no water, no trees, no cover.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Your Springfield carbine jams after every shot — the copper cartridge expands in the heat. You pry it out with a knife while riders close the distance.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: All 210 men on that ridge died in under an hour. Custer split his command, attacked what he couldn't see, and gave you a jammed rifle to fight it with.
