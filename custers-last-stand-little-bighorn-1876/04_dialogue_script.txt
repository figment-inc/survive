## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: You ride into a valley with two hundred sixty men. Your commander just split the regiment into three pieces. You would not want to be at Little Bighorn, 1876.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Your commander wants to attack before the village can scatter. You're ordered forward, but nobody knows the camp stretches three miles along the river — the largest gathering of Lakota and Cheyenne.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Your column is pushed back to a ridge. You form a skirmish line — the high ground is your only advantage.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: All 210 men on that ridge died in under an hour. Custer split his command, attacked what he couldn't see, and gave you a jammed rifle to fight it with.
