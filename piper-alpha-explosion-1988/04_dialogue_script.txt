## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A safety valve is removed for maintenance. The paperwork sits on a desk — nobody tells the night shift. You would not want to be on Piper Alpha, 1988.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You restart the pump. Gas blasts through the open pipe and finds an ignition source in seconds.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You feel the heat double. Two connected rigs keep pumping oil straight into the fire — their managers wait for permission to shut down.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The platform melts beneath your feet. You jump thirty metres into burning sea.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: One handover note, never passed on. You and 166 others died waiting for someone to read it.
