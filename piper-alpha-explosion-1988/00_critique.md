One optional note — not a required fix:

**Clip 02:** "Gas blasts through the open pipe and finds an ignition source in seconds" is mechanically clear but the viewer is an observer here. If you wanted to push Gut Punch toward a 9, you could add a half-second of sensory dread: "You restart the pump. Gas screams through the open pipe — ignition in seconds." The word "screams" puts sound in the viewer's head and costs zero additional words. But this is a polish note, not a problem. Ship it.
