## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A mountain of coal waste sits directly above a primary school. The local council asked to have it moved — the Coal Board said no. You would not want to be in Aberfan, 1966.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: For twenty years, miners dumped waste on the hillside above the village. Nobody checked that the tip sat on top of natural springs.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Heavy rain saturates the base. At 9:15 in the morning, the tip liquefies and slides — a black wave moving thirty miles per hour straight downhill.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: It hits Pantglas Junior School mid-lesson. Classrooms fill floor to ceiling in seconds. You can't dig fast enough.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: 116 of the 144 dead were children. The Coal Board knew the springs were there — and put the tip on top of them anyway.
