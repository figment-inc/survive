## LINE-LEVEL CRITIQUE

### Word Count — 7/10

**Problem:** 94 words — 4 over the sweet spot ceiling. Not a crisis but worth a trim pass.
**Fix:** Clip 02 is the loosest. "You live below twenty years of dumped coal waste — sitting on natural springs nobody checked." can lose a few words:
**Rewrite:** "You live below twenty years of dumped coal waste — piled on natural springs nobody checked." (Saves 1 word; alternatively, trim "directly" from Clip 01 — "A mountain of coal waste sits above your children's school" saves 1 more.)

### Closure — 7/10

**Failing line (Clip 05):** "The Coal Board knew the springs were there — and put the tip on top of them anyway. 116 of the 144 dead were children."
**Problem:** This is strong but structured as accusation + stat. It's close to summary. The stat "116 of the 144" does land as a gut punch rather than a wrap-up signal because the ratio is horrifying — but the opening clause ("The Coal Board knew") announces a conclusion rather than delivering a revelation mid-stride. It's a 7, not a 5 — it works, but it could hit harder.
**Fix:** Flip the structure so the human cost arrives first and the institutional betrayal is the final word the viewer processes: "116 of the 144 dead were children — buried by a tip the Coal Board knew sat on springs, and refused to move." Now the revelation is the last thing ringing in the viewer's ear.

### You-As-Subject — 7/10

**Failing line (Clip 05):** "The Coal Board knew the springs were there — and put the tip on top of them anyway."
**Problem:** Clip 05 has zero "you" — it shifts entirely to third-person institutional voice. The viewer is ejected from their body at the most critical moment.
**Failing line (Clip 02):** "You live below twenty years of dumped coal waste — sitting on natural springs nobody checked."
**Problem:** "You live below" is static. You exist, but you don't act or experience anything.
**Fix for Clip 02:** "You send your children to school every morning — directly below twenty years of dumped coal waste, piled on springs nobody checked." Now "you" is doing something, and the parental stakes sharpen.
**Fix for Clip 05:** "Your children are under that slurry — buried by a tip the Coal Board knew sat on springs, and refused to move." Keeps "you" in the frame through the closer.
