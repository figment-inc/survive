## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A mountain of coal waste sits directly above your children's school. The council asked to move it — the Coal Board said no. You would not want to be in Aberfan, 1966.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You live below twenty years of dumped coal waste — sitting on natural springs nobody checked.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Heavy rain saturates the base. At 9:15, you hear a roar — the tip liquefies and slides, a black wave moving thirty miles per hour toward your school.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The Coal Board knew the springs were there — and put the tip on top of them anyway. 116 of the 144 dead were children.
