## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A brewing vat holding 135,000 gallons of beer just burst — and you live one street away. You would not want to be in London, 1814.

## Clip 02 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You hear the first hoop snap. The pressure blows out neighboring vats — a quarter million gallons hit the street in seconds.

## Clip 03 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You live below ground. Entire families do — it's the only rent you can afford. The beer pours down the stairwells before you can stand.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Nine people drowned in basement rooms. The brewery called it an act of God — and paid nothing.
