## Clip 01 (0:00-0:08) — The Hook
NARRATOR: The fire starts in a shop. It burns for nine days. A million people have nowhere to go. You would not want to be in Rome, 64 AD.

## Clip 02 (0:08-0:16) — Routine
NARRATOR: You haul amphorae up three flights of wooden stairs. The tenement block sways when the wind picks up. Forty families share one exit.

## Clip 03 (0:16-0:24) — The Detail
NARRATOR: Rome stacks the poor in timber. Six, seven storeys — illegal, but no one enforces the codes. The alleys between are arm-width.

## Clip 04 (0:24-0:32) — The Turn
NARRATOR: July nineteenth. An oil merchant's stall catches near the Circus Maximus. The wind funnels through the valley between the hills.

## Clip 05 (0:32-0:40) — Escalation
NARRATOR: You grab your children and run. The alley is already full. The fire moves faster than the crowd.

## Clip 06 (0:40-0:48) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Tenements collapse inward, blocking the streets with burning timber. You cannot go back. The route ahead is smoke.

## Clip 07 (0:48-0:56) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Ten of fourteen districts burn. The stone temples crack from heat. You shelter in a field and watch your neighbourhood become ash.

## Clip 08 (0:56-1:04) — The Wait
NARRATOR: Nine days. The fire dies, reignites, shifts direction. You do not sleep. You breathe through wet cloth that dries within the hour.

## Clip 09 (1:04-1:12) — The Human Gesture
NARRATOR: Nero opens his private gardens to the homeless. He distributes grain. Then he starts to build.

## Clip 10 (1:12-1:20) — Aftermath
NARRATOR: Where your home stood, surveyors mark the foundations of a golden palace. One hundred acres. For one man.

## Clip 11 (1:20-1:28) — The Number
NARRATOR: Rome needed someone to blame. Nero chose a sect so small most Romans had never heard of them — the Christians.

## Clip 12 (1:28-1:36) — Callback
NARRATOR: He opened his gardens to you. Then he filled them with crosses and set them on fire for light.
