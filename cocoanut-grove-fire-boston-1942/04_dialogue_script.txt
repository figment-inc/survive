## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A thousand people are packed inside the Cocoanut Grove nightclub. The owner has bolted every exit to stop people skipping bar tabs. You would not want to be in Boston, 1942.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: One revolving door is your only way out. The walls are draped in fake silk and paper palm trees — decorations no one ever fireproofed.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A busboy near you lights a match to find a loose bulb. You see flames race across the ceiling before you can stand.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: 492 people died in fifteen minutes — every exit bolted shut to save a few dollars on bar tabs.
