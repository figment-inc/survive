## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Edo is the largest city on earth — built almost entirely from wood and paper. A monk burns a cursed kimono, and the wind carries the embers. You would not want to be in Edo, 1657.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Hundred-thousand wooden buildings packed so tightly you can touch both walls of an alley. There are no firebreaks.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The fire burns for three days. You flee south — but the gates in the city walls are locked to prevent looting.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Tens of thousands crowd the riverbanks. The bridges are already burning. You jump. The water is January cold.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The walls were built to keep armies out. They kept a hundred thousand people in.
