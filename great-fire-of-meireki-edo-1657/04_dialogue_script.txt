## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Edo is the largest city on earth — built almost entirely from wood and paper. A monk burns a cursed kimono, and the wind carries the embers. You would not want to be in Edo, 1657.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You live in one of a hundred thousand wooden buildings packed so tightly you can touch both walls of an alley. There are no firebreaks.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The fire jumps roof to roof for three days — nothing between buildings to stop it. You flee south, but the gates in the city walls are locked to prevent looting.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The walls were built to keep armies out. They kept a hundred thousand people in.
