## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: London burns coal to stay warm. One freezing week, the smoke can't rise — the air itself turns poisonous. You would not want to be in London, 1952.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You light your coal fire like everyone else. A layer of warm air seals the city like a lid — your smoke has nowhere to go.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Visibility drops to one foot. You can't see your own hands. Sulfur dioxide mixes with moisture and becomes acid in your lungs.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You survive — twelve thousand of your neighbors don't. The government calls it fog, and takes four years to ban the coal that killed them.
