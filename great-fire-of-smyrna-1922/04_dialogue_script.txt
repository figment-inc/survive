## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Greek army collapses. You're one of a hundred thousand civilians pushed to the sea. You would not want to be in Smyrna, 1922.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Allied warships sit in the harbor. Their governments want oil concessions from the winning side — their orders say do not intervene.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Turkish soldiers set fire to the Armenian quarter. It drives you to the water's edge. You wave at the ships. A searchlight finds you.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You stand in the surf. Heat at your back. The warships won't lower a single boat.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: You scream toward the ships. Their crews play music to drown it out.
