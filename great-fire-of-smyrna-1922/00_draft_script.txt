## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Greek army is retreating and a hundred thousand refugees have nowhere to go but the waterfront. You would not want to be in Smyrna, 1922.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Allied warships sit in the harbor. Their sailors watch from the rails. They have orders not to intervene.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Fire breaks out in the Armenian quarter and spreads toward the sea — pushing you and every refugee to the water's edge.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You're standing in the surf. The heat behind you is unbearable. The warships ahead won't lower a single boat.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The ships were close enough to hear you scream. Their crews played music to drown it out.
