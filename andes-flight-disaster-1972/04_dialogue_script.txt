## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your plane clips a mountain and crashes in the Andes. Rescue teams search for eight days — but the white fuselage is invisible against snow. They declare everyone dead. You would not want to be on Flight 571, 1972.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The pilot flew into clouds and misjudged the pass. You're stranded at twelve thousand feet with no food, no heat, and no radio.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You're asleep when the avalanche hits. Snow fills the fuselage — you dig out, but eight others don't. You find a transistor radio and hear that the search has been called off. No one is coming.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Two of you hike ten days through the mountains. Sixteen survive out of forty-five — rescued only because you saved yourselves.
