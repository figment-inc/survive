## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your plane clips a mountain and crashes in the Andes. Rescue teams search for eight days — then declare everyone dead. You would not want to be on Flight 571, 1972.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The pilot flew into clouds and misjudged the pass. You're stranded at twelve thousand feet with no food, no heat, and no radio.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: An avalanche buries the fuselage at night. Eight more die. The survivors realize no one is coming.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Two months in. You eat the dead to stay alive — because there is nothing else.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Two men hiked ten days through the mountains to find help. Sixteen survived out of forty-five — rescued only because they saved themselves.
