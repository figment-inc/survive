## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Edmund Fitzgerald is the largest ship on the Great Lakes. Her captain reports a fence rail down and two vents lost — the hull is open to the lake. You would not want to be on Lake Superior, 1975.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: November waves hit thirty-five feet. Water pours through every gap. The pumps are running full, but you're listing.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A following ship watches you on radar. You radio that you're holding your own — then your radar blip vanishes.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: No distress call. No time. The bow digs into a wave and the lake swallows you whole.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Twenty-nine men gone in seconds. They never found out what broke — because no one required the Fitzgerald to carry a voyage data recorder.
