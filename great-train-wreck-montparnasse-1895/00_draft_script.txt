## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your train has no working brakes — and the station ends at a wall. You would not want to be at Montparnasse, Paris, 1895.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The driver is running late. Company rules punish delays with fines, so he comes in fast, counting on the Westinghouse air brake. It fails.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You blow through the buffer stop, across the concourse, and through the station wall.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The locomotive hangs two stories above the street, dangling by its own steam lines. Below you, a newsstand where a woman was standing.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: One woman on the sidewalk died — crushed by falling masonry because a driver feared a late fine more than his speed.
