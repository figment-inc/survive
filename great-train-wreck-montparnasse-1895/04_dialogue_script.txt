## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your train has no working brakes — and the station ends at a wall. You would not want to be at Montparnasse, Paris, 1895.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're running late. The company fines drivers for delays, so you come in fast, counting on the Westinghouse air brake. It fails.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You hit the buffer stop. It doesn't slow you. You plow across the concourse and through the terminal wall.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: One woman on the sidewalk died — crushed by falling masonry because a driver feared a late fine more than his speed.
