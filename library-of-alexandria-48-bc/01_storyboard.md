**Slug**: `library-of-alexandria-48-bc`
**Setting**: The Royal Quarter of Alexandria, Egypt, during Julius Caesar's siege of the city, 48 BC.
**Hook**: The largest collection of human knowledge on Earth burns — and nobody set fire to it on purpose.
