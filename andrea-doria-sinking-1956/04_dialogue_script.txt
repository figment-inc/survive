## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your ship has radar — the first generation built to prevent collisions. You would not want to be on the Andrea Doria, 1956.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Dense fog off Nantucket. You and the Stockholm both see each other on radar, but each captain assumes the other will pass correctly — and neither radios to confirm.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: At eleven miles, you're closing head-on. Both ships turn the same direction. You're now aimed straight at each other's hulls.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You take an ice-breaking bow thirty feet into your starboard side. Your ship lists so far that half the lifeboats can't launch.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Forty-six people died on a ship with enough lifeboats for everyone. Two radars, two crews, and nobody picked up the radio.
