## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Rome's streets are six feet wide, lined with wooden apartments stacked seven stories high. A fire starts in an oil shop. You would not want to be in Rome, 64 AD.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Rome's vigiles carry buckets and blankets. You watch them soak a doorframe, but they cannot stop a burning building from igniting the one three feet away.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Fire funnels through the tight streets like a chimney. You run, but every route leads to another burning block.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Nero blames the Christians among you and executes hundreds. But it was Rome's own streets — built too narrow, too tall, too cheap to survive a single spark.
