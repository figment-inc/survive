# The London Blitz — 1940

**Slug**: `london-blitz-1940`
**Setting**: London, England during the German strategic bombing campaign, September 1940.
**Hook**: For fifty-seven consecutive nights, the bombs never stop falling — and the safest place in the city will crush more people than it saves.

**Style Description**: Deep blackout-navy and charcoal grey palette pierced by harsh searchlight white cones and incendiary orange-amber fire glow. Flat atmospheric haze of smoke and brick dust fills every frame, muting distant details into grey silhouettes. The lighting is split — dark navy sky above, warm destructive orange below — creating a two-tone world. Recurring visual motifs: criss-crossing white searchlight beams against the dark sky, and drifting flat ember particles in every exterior frame.
