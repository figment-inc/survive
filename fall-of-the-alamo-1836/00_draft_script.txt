## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Santa Anna's army outnumbers you twenty to one. Your commander chose to defend a crumbling church with no roof. You would not want to be at the Alamo, 1836.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Colonel Travis drew a line in the dirt — stay and fight or leave. Nearly every man stayed, betting reinforcements would arrive.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Thirteen days you hold the walls. The reinforcements never come. Santa Anna raises a red flag — no quarter, no surrender accepted.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Two thousand soldiers hit every wall at once before dawn. You fire once, maybe twice. Then they're over the top.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: All 200 defenders died in ninety minutes — waiting for help that was never sent.
