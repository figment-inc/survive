## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Santa Anna's army outnumbers you twenty to one. Your commander chose to defend a crumbling church with no roof. You would not want to be at the Alamo, 1836.

## Clip 02 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Thirteen days you hold the walls. No reinforcements reach you. You see Santa Anna raise a red flag — no quarter, no surrender accepted.

## Clip 03 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Two thousand soldiers hit every wall at once before dawn. You fire once, maybe twice. Then they're over the top.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: All two hundred defenders die in ninety minutes — because one general thirty miles away decided you weren't worth saving.
