## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Mount Etna opens a fissure a mile long, and lava heads straight for Catania — a city of twenty thousand behind medieval walls. You would not want to be in Catania, 1669.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The lava moves slow enough to watch. Townspeople build stone barriers to divert it. It works — until the neighbors whose homes you've redirected it toward tear your walls down.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Lava hits the city walls. You'd think that stops it — instead, the walls dam it into a rising lake of molten rock.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: It spills over the top and pours into the streets. There is nowhere left to send it.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Lava buried half the city. The walls built to protect you became the bowl that filled.
