## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: You live in Catania — a city of twenty thousand behind medieval walls. Mount Etna opens a fissure a mile long, and lava heads straight for you. You would not want to be in Catania, 1669.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The lava moves slow enough to watch. You build stone barriers to divert it — and the neighbors whose homes you've redirected it toward tear your walls down.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You retreat behind the city walls. The walls hold — and dam the lava into a rising lake of molten rock pooling against your streets.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Lava buried half the city. The walls you built to keep invaders out became the bowl that held the molten rock in.
