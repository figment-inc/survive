## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A blizzard traps your train on a mountain ledge for six days. The railroad won't let you walk out. You would not want to be in Wellington, Washington, 1910.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Snow buries the tracks in both directions. You're parked on a narrow shelf cut into the mountainside — a steep slope above, a gorge below.

## Clip 03 — Turn
NARRATOR: On the sixth night, the blizzard turns to rain. Rain on ten feet of snow loosens everything holding the mountainside together.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: A slab of snow half a mile wide breaks free. It sweeps your train off the ledge and into the ravine — still full of sleeping passengers.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Passengers begged to hike out days earlier. The railroad said the train was safer. Ninety-six people died in their bunks.
