## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Athens crams everyone behind its walls to survive a Spartan siege. Then a plague breaks out inside. You would not want to be in Athens, 430 BC.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Pericles ordered the countryside evacuated. Farmers, families, livestock — all packed into a city built for half as many people. You sleep in makeshift shacks between the Long Walls.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The sickness starts in your eyes and throat, then moves downward through every organ. No one knows what causes it, so no one can stop it.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Bodies stack in temples. The dying crawl to public fountains and collapse. Laws break down — people spend everything, expecting to die tomorrow.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: A quarter of the population died — trapped behind the very walls meant to save them.
