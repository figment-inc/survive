## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Your army is four hundred miles north, fighting in Canada. There is almost no one left between the coast and your capital. You would not want to be in Washington, 1814.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Four thousand British veterans march inland from the Chesapeake. You line up with the militia at Bladensburg — your line breaks and runs before a single volley.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You hear drums before you see the column. You've already fled. The Redcoats walk into an empty city — dinner still warm on the President's table.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Your nation sent its entire army north to invade Canada. The British walked through your front door unopposed.
