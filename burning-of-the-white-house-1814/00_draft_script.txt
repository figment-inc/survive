## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: America's entire army is fighting on the Canadian border. There's almost no one left to defend the capital. You would not want to be in Washington, 1814.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Four thousand British veterans march inland from the Chesapeake. The militia sent to stop them breaks and runs before a single volley.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You watch redcoats walk into an empty city. Dinner is still warm on the table in the President's House.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: They stack the furniture, pour rocket powder, and light it. The Capitol, the Treasury, the Library — everything burns at once.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The most powerful young nation on earth sent its army north to invade Canada. The British walked through the front door unopposed.
