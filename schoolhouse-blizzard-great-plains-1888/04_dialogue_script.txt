## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: You send your children to school in shirt sleeves — it's January, but it feels like spring. The temperature drops forty degrees in minutes. You would not want to be on the Great Plains, 1888.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: An Arctic front crashes south without warning. You can't see the building next door. Your children sit in one-room schoolhouses miles away, and you have no way to reach them.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You tie your students together with rope and walk into the white — or you keep them inside, but the coal runs out.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You sent them to school in shirt sleeves because it felt like spring. Two hundred and thirty-five of your children never came home.
