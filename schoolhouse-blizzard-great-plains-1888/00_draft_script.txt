## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: It's a warm January morning, so you send your children to school without coats. By afternoon, the temperature drops forty degrees in minutes. You would not want to be on the Great Plains, 1888.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: A wall of ice and wind hits with zero visibility. Teachers can't see the building next door. Your children are inside one-room schoolhouses miles from home.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Some teachers tie their students together with rope and walk into the white. Others keep them inside — but the coal runs out.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Minus forty with sixty-mile-per-hour winds. You step outside and lose direction in three steps.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Two hundred thirty-five people froze to death — most of them children, sent to school that morning in shirt sleeves because it felt like spring.
