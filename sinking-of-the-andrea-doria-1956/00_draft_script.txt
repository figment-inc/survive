## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Stockholm has radar — built to prevent exactly this collision. The officer reads it wrong. You would not want to be on the Andrea Doria, 1956.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Dense fog off Nantucket. Both ships see each other on radar, and both turn — but into each other's path.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The Stockholm's ice-hardened bow punches straight through your starboard side. The Andrea Doria lists so sharply that half the lifeboats can't launch.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You climb uphill on a tilting deck. The boats on your side hang over open water, unreachable.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Forty-six people died — on a ship with enough lifeboats for everyone, because the collision made half of them useless.
