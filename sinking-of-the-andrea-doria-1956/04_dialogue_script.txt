## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Stockholm has radar — built to prevent exactly this collision. The officer reads it wrong. You would not want to be on the Andrea Doria, 1956.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're in dense fog off Nantucket. Your crew sees the Stockholm on radar and turns — but the Stockholm turns too, directly into your path.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You feel the Stockholm's ice-hardened bow punch straight through your starboard side. Your ship lists so sharply that half the lifeboats can't launch.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Forty-six people died — on a ship with enough lifeboats for everyone, because the collision made half of them useless.
