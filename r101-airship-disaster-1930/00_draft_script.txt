## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The airship R101 is half a million cubic feet short of lift — so the British government orders her flown anyway. You would not want to be on the R101, 1930.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Engineers sliced her in half and added an extra gas bag to fix the weight problem. The outer fabric leaks hydrogen in rain.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: It's raining over France. You're flying low, nose dipping. Hydrogen is seeping through the wet canvas above your head.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The bow drops into a hillside at Beauvais. The hydrogen ignites. The frame collapses around you in seconds.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Forty-eight of fifty-four aboard died — because no one would tell the Air Minister his airship wasn't ready to fly.
