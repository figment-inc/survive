## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Mud is surging up through your oil well and the crew disables the alarm so it won't wake anyone. You would not want to be on the Deepwater Horizon, 2010.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're drilling the deepest offshore well in history. The only thing between you and a blowout is a cement seal at the bottom — and pressure tests say it's failing.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You watch your engineers flag the test. Managers override them and call it good enough — and you're told to keep drilling. Gas rockets up the pipe at thousands of pounds per square inch.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Eleven men died. Oil poured for 87 days. Every safeguard existed — and every one was overruled.
