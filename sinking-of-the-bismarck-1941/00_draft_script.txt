## Clip 01 (0:00-0:07) — Hook
NARRATOR: The Bismarck is the most powerful battleship in the Atlantic — and the entire British Navy is hunting it. You would not want to be on the Bismarck, 1941.

## Clip 02 (0:07-0:14) — Setup
NARRATOR: You just sank the HMS Hood, Britain's most famous warship, in six minutes. One thousand four hundred men dead. Now every British vessel within a thousand miles has one order — find you and sink you.

## Clip 03 (0:14-0:21) — Escalation
NARRATOR: You almost escape. The Bismarck is fast enough to outrun the fleet. Then a single torpedo from a tiny biplane hits your rudder and jams it hard to port. Your ship can only sail in circles.

## Clip 04 (0:21-0:28) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: By morning, two battleships close in and fire at point-blank range. Shells punch through your armor every few seconds. You can't steer, you can't run, you can't fight back.

## Clip 05 (0:28-0:35) — Ending
NARRATOR: Over two thousand men went into the Atlantic. The British picked up only 114 — then left, afraid of submarines. The mightiest warship ever built, stopped by one lucky torpedo to the rudder.
