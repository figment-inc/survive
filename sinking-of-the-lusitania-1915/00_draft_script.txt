## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A German U-boat torpedoes the largest passenger liner crossing the Atlantic. She sinks in eighteen minutes. You would not want to be on the Lusitania, 1915.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The German embassy took out newspaper ads warning you not to board. The British Admiralty promised a naval escort — then quietly cancelled it.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: One torpedo hits. Then a second explosion rips the bow open from inside — munitions the ship was never supposed to be carrying.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The list is so steep half the lifeboats can't launch. You slide across the deck into freezing water.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: They told you the ship was too fast to be hit. She was sailing at three-quarter speed — to save coal.
