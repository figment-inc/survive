## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A German U-boat torpedoes the largest passenger liner crossing the Atlantic. She sinks in eighteen minutes. You would not want to be on the Lusitania, 1915.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You see the German embassy's warning in the morning paper. You board anyway — the British Admiralty promised a naval escort. They quietly cancelled it.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You feel one torpedo hit. Then a second explosion rips the bow open beneath you — munitions the ship was never supposed to be carrying.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You were told the ship was too fast to be hit. You were sailing at three-quarter speed — to save coal.
