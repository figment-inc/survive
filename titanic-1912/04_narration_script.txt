# You Wouldn't Wanna Be — Sinking of the Titanic (1912)
# Narration Script (extracted from storyboard)

---

## Beat 1: The Hook

**Clip 01** — The Hook
"What happens if you wake up on the Titanic two hours before it hits the iceberg?"

---

## Beat 2: The Immersion

**Clip 02** — Third-Class Bunk
"You open your eyes in a third-class bunk. The sheets smell like coal dust. A baby is crying three doors down. It is April fourteenth, nineteen twelve."

**Clip 03** — The Corridors
"You know exactly what is about to happen. You push through the corridor. You need to find a lifeboat. But you are six decks below the boat deck. And the gates are locked."

**Clip 04** — The Warning
"You grab a steward. You tell him the ship is going to sink. He smiles. He says the Titanic cannot sink. Everyone says that."

---

## Beat 3: The Attempt

**Clip 05** — The Impact
"Eleven forty PM. You feel it. A shudder. Like the ship just exhaled. The engines stop. The silence is worse than the impact."

**Clip 06** — Water Rising
"Water appears. It does not rush. It creeps. Ankle-deep in the mail room. Then knee-deep. Then the lights flicker."

**Clip 07** — The Gates
"You run for the stairs. The gates are still locked. A mother is pushing her children through the bars. A crewman fumbles with keys that do not fit."

---

## Beat 4: The Catastrophe

**Clip 08** — The Boat Deck
"On the boat deck, it is chaos disguised as calm. The band is playing. Women and children first. But there are not enough boats. There were never enough boats."

**Clip 09** — The Math
"The bow drops. The stern rises. Fifteen hundred people realize at the same moment that the math does not work. Twenty lifeboats. Two thousand two hundred passengers."

**Clip 10** — The Break
"The ship breaks in half. The sound is like nothing you have ever heard. Steel screaming. A thousand voices in the dark."

**Clip 11** — The Water
"The water is twenty-eight degrees. You have fifteen minutes before your body shuts down. You can see the lifeboats. They are not coming back."

---

## Beat 5: The Cliffhanger

**Clip 12** — The Reckoning
"Two hours and forty minutes. That is how long it took. The unsinkable ship. Gone."

**Clip 13** — The Final Frame
"Seven hundred and ten people survived. You were not one of them."
