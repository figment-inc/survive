## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Dresden has no air defenses. The Allies know this, so they send two waves — the first to start fires, the second to kill the firefighters. You would not want to be in Dresden, 1945.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The city is packed with refugees fleeing the Eastern Front. Hundreds of thousands of civilians with nowhere left to go.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The first wave drops incendiaries. Thousands of small fires merge into one — a firestorm. Superheated air rises, sucking hurricane-force winds inward through every street.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Three hours later, the second wave arrives. You're in the open, fighting fires. The bombs fall on you.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Twenty-five thousand people burned in a city that couldn't shoot back — hit twice to make sure the rescuers died too.
