## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Dresden has no air defenses. The Allies know this, so they send two waves — the first to start fires, the second to kill the firefighters. You would not want to be in Dresden, 1945.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You're one of hundreds of thousands of refugees packed into the city, fleeing the Eastern Front, with nowhere left to go.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The first wave drops incendiaries. Thousands of small fires merge into one firestorm around you. The air superheats, and you feel hurricane-force winds tearing inward through every street.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: Twenty-five thousand people burned in a city that couldn't shoot back — hit twice to make sure the rescuers died too.
