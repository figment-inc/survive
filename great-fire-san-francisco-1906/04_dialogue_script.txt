## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: San Francisco's fire chief is crushed by a falling chimney in the first minutes of the earthquake. Nobody left in charge knows where the water mains are. You would not want to be in San Francisco, 1906.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You wrench open the nearest hydrant. Dust spits from the valve — nothing. The quake has snapped every main under the city.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The Army dynamites buildings ahead of the fire to stop it. You feel the blast. They used black powder instead of proper charges, and everything they blow apart ignites.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The earthquake broke the pipes. The rescue broke the city — you lost more to dynamite than to the quake itself.
