## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: San Francisco's fire chief is crushed by a falling chimney in the first minutes of the earthquake. Nobody left in charge knows where the water mains are. You would not want to be in San Francisco, 1906.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The quake has snapped every water main under the city. Firefighters connect hoses and nothing comes out.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The Army decides to stop the fire by dynamiting buildings in its path. They use black powder instead of proper charges — and ignite everything they blow apart.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You watch the firebreaks become new fires. Three days. Five square miles burn.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: The earthquake broke the pipes. The rescue broke the city. More was destroyed by dynamite than by the quake itself.
