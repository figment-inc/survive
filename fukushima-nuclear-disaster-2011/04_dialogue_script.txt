## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The backup generators keeping your reactor cool are in the basement. The tsunami is forty feet tall. You would not want to be at Fukushima, 2011.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Your reactors shut down automatically — exactly as designed. But the fuel inside still generates massive heat. Without cooling, you watch it melt through its own containment.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The wave floods the basement. Every generator drowns. The lights go out. You're venting hydrogen to relieve pressure — and hydrogen explodes.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: The reactors survived the earthquake. They survived the tsunami. They couldn't survive the decision to put the backup generators below sea level.
