## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A ferry carrying four thousand people has no passenger manifest — nobody knows who's on board. You would not want to be on the Doña Paz, 1987.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You board a ship licensed for 1,493 — the company sells every ticket it can. More than four thousand pack every deck, and there are two life jackets for every hundred people.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: No lookout is posted. You feel the impact before you see it — an oil tanker in the dark. Fuel spills across the water and ignites, and the sea around you is burning.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You left no name on any list. No one ever confirmed how many died — or how many were never reported missing at all.
