## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A ferry carrying four thousand people has no passenger manifest — nobody knows who's on board. You would not want to be on the Doña Paz, 1987.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The ship is licensed for 1,493 passengers. Tonight, more than four thousand crowd every deck. There are two life jackets for every hundred people.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: An oil tanker collides in the dark. Fuel spills across the water and ignites — the sea itself is burning.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You jump and the ocean is on fire. There is nowhere to swim.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Rescue ships arrived eight hours later. Only 24 survived — and because there was no manifest, the Philippines never learned exactly how many were lost.
