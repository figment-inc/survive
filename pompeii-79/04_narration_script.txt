# Narration Script — Destruction of Pompeii (79 AD)

**Total words**: ~215
**Target runtime**: ~65 seconds at 3 words/second

---

## Beat 1: The Hook

**Clip 01 — The Hook**
"What happens if you wake up in Pompeii three hours before Vesuvius buries it forever?"

---

## Beat 2: The Immersion

**Clip 02 — Sensory Arrival**
"You wake up on hot stone. The air tastes like sulfur. A man grabs your arm. He is shouting in Latin. You don't understand a word."

**Clip 03 — The Mountain**
"Then you see the mountain. And you remember everything."

---

## Beat 3: The Attempt

**Clip 04 — Warning the Crowd**
"You grab a merchant. You point at the mountain. You try to make them understand. They laugh. A woman pulls her child away from you. No one listens."

**Clip 05 — The Realization**
"That is when you realize. No one is going to believe you."

**Clip 06 — Running for the Gate**
"You run for the Herculaneum Gate. You push through twenty thousand people who do not know they are about to die. The gate is jammed. A soldier shoves you back. The ground shakes again."

---

## Beat 4: The Catastrophe

**Clip 07 — Eruption**
"Vesuvius tears itself apart. A column of ash rockets fifty thousand feet into the sky. Pumice falls like hail. You are having a very bad day."

**Clip 08 — Darkness**
"The sky disappears. Day becomes night. You cannot see your own hands."

**Clip 09 — Pyroclastic Surge**
"Then comes the surge. Four hundred degrees. A hundred miles per hour. There is nowhere to run. There was never anywhere to run."

---

## Beat 5: The Cliffhanger

**Clip 10 — Found**
"In seventeen forty-eight, they find this city exactly as it fell. Bread still in the ovens. Bodies where they dropped. And you. Still reaching for the gate."
