## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Aleppo's citadel survived every army for centuries. The ground beneath it hasn't moved in so long, no one builds for earthquakes. You would not want to be in Aleppo, 1138.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Houses are heavy stone stacked on mud mortar — strong against swords, brittle against shaking. The city is packed tight, wall to wall.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: The first tremor hits at dawn. You run outside — but the streets are so narrow falling walls block every path.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The citadel collapses. Stone roofs pancake onto sleeping families. Entire neighborhoods fold inward in seconds.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: 230,000 people died — in a city that never imagined the ground could move.
