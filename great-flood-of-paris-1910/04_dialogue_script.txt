## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Seine doesn't overflow its banks — it rises through your floor. Your sewers, your metro, your basements — all built directly into the river's water table. You would not want to be in Paris, 1910.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: Six weeks of rain saturate the ground. You wake to water pooling on your basement floor — no broken pipe, no leak. It's pushing up through the drain itself.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You sandbag the riverbank, but the water is already behind you. The metro floods. Power stations go dark. The pumps keeping the sewers flowing — they stop.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: You spent a century building underground. The river simply followed your pipes back in.
