## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Los Angeles builds a dam to steal water from a valley four hundred miles away. You would not want to be below the St. Francis Dam, 1928.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You live below a dam one man designed alone — no independent review. The canyon wall is ancient landslide debris that dissolves when wet.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You watch muddy water pour from the base for weeks. Mulholland inspects it and tells you it's harmless.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: One man approved the site, the design, and the leaks. You are asleep downstream when his dam collapses — so are four hundred others.
