## Clip 01 (0:00-0:08) — The Hook
NARRATOR: The king is watching from the shore. His flagship rolls, and five hundred men drown within a bowshot of land. You would not want to be on the Mary Rose, 1545.

## Clip 02 (0:08-0:16) — Routine
NARRATOR: You are a gunner on the pride of the Tudor fleet. Thirty-four years she has served the crown. You know every plank, every port, every rope.

## Clip 03 (0:16-0:24) — False Safety
NARRATOR: A French armada has entered the Solent — two hundred ships against your eighty. But you have the guns, the tide, and the wind is turning in your favour.

## Clip 04 (0:24-0:32) — The Detail
NARRATOR: Your lower gunports sit sixteen inches above the waterline. Sixteen inches — the length of your forearm. They are open. They must be, because you are firing.

## Clip 05 (0:32-0:40) — The Turn
NARRATOR: The order comes to turn hard to starboard and bring the opposite guns to bear. The ship heels. The open ports dip toward the sea —

## Clip 06 (0:40-0:48) — Escalation
NARRATOR: — and the Channel pours in. Not a wave. Not a storm. Just a turn taken with the ports left wide. The water reaches the gun deck in seconds.

## Clip 07 (0:48-0:56) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You scramble for the hatch, but anti-boarding netting is lashed across the deck. It was strung to keep the French out. Now it keeps you in.

## Clip 08 (0:56-1:04) — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: The ship rolls past forty degrees. Cannons break loose and slide across the deck. Men tangled in the netting go under with the hull still above them.

## Clip 09 (1:04-1:12) — The Human Gesture
NARRATOR: On shore, Henry watches. He hears the wife of the admiral cry out. He says nothing. There is nothing to say.

## Clip 10 (1:12-1:20) — Aftermath
NARRATOR: The masts remain above the surface, flags still flying. Beneath them, five hundred men are trapped inside a ship that sank in less than a minute.

## Clip 11 (1:20-1:28) — The Number
NARRATOR: Fewer than thirty-five survive. Not because the sea was rough. Not because the enemy struck a blow.

## Clip 12 (1:28-1:36) — Callback
NARRATOR: You opened the ports to fight. You strung the nets to survive. Both killed you, and the king watched every second of it.
