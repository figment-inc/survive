## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The Cocoanut Grove nightclub holds 460 people. Tonight there are over a thousand inside, and the exit doors are bolted shut. You would not want to be in Boston, 1942.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The owner locked the side exits to stop people from skipping their bar tabs. One main revolving door is your way out.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A busboy lights a match to fix a lightbulb near fake palm decorations. The fabric canopy catches — fire races across the ceiling faster than you can stand up.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: You rush the revolving door. Bodies crush against it from both sides and it jams. The locked exits don't budge.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: 492 people died in fifteen minutes. Every locked door was a choice someone made to save a few dollars on bar tabs.
