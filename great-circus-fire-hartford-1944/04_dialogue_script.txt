## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: Ringling Brothers waterproofed their big top with paraffin dissolved in gasoline. Six thousand people sit under it. You would not want to be in Hartford, 1944.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: The tent has no fire exits — just narrow animal chutes blocked by steel runways. Your only way out is the way you came in.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: You see a small flame on the sidewall. The paraffin-soaked canvas doesn't burn — it melts. Liquid fire drips onto the seats around you.

## Clip 04 — Ending
NARRATOR: 167 people died — because someone waterproofed your tent with gasoline and locked the exits behind animal cages.
