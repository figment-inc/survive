## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: A mob of nine hundred Parisians is charging your fortress. You have thirty Swiss guards and eighty retired soldiers to stop them. You would not want to be at the Bastille, 1789.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You lower the drawbridge to negotiate. The crowd takes that as surrender and floods the courtyard.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: Your men open fire. Ninety-eight people drop. But the crowd doesn't retreat — they drag up stolen cannons and aim them at your gate.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Your own soldiers mutiny and unlock the doors. The mob is inside.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: You lowered the bridge to talk. They remembered you opened fire. The governor's head ended up on a pike before sunset.
