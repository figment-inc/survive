## Clip 01 — Hook
NARRATOR: The German army fights five months for Stalingrad. Hitler won't let them leave. You would not want to be in Stalingrad, 1942.

## Clip 02 — Setup
NARRATOR: You fight room by room, floor by floor. By November, you hold the city.

## Clip 03 — Escalation
NARRATOR: A million Soviets surround you. You're ordered to hold — the Luftwaffe will fly in supplies. They deliver a tenth of what you need.

## Clip 04 — Catastrophe
NARRATOR: Minus thirty. Your rations drop to a slice of bread. You freeze in your trench.

## Clip 05 — Ending
NARRATOR: Of 300,000 trapped, 6,000 came home — because one man wouldn't say retreat.
